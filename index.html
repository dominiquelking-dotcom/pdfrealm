Live tools in your browser

Pick a tool, enter your information, and export a clean PDF. No templates required— you bring the content, PDFRealm handles the file.

PDF Studio (coming soon)

A focused workspace for viewing, annotating, and signing PDFs directly in your browser. You’ll be able to drag in a file, drop signatures or notes where you need them, and export a ready-to-send PDF.

While this workspace is being built, you can still sign and generate PDFs using the other tools above.

Live preview

Once PDF Studio is active, this area will show a live view of the PDF you’re working on with markers for signatures and annotations.

Quick Sign

Drop in a PDF, type a name, and place a simple signature on a single page. Ideal when you just need a quick, basic sign-off.

Note: this is a simple visual signature tool and is not a substitute for a jurisdiction-specific legal e-signature system.

Signature preview

After you upload a PDF and enter your name, a preview of the signed page will appear here before you export.

Invoice generator

Create a clean, simple invoice PDF for any service or product. Enter the details and export as a ready-to-send document.

Invoice preview

As you build out your backend PDF layouts, this panel can be wired to show a live preview of the invoice before exporting.

Receipt generator

Issue a simple receipt PDF for payments you’ve received. Great for personal records or small business use.

Receipt preview

A structured summary of the payer, amount, method, and reason will be laid out in the final PDF.

Paystub generator

Build a simple paystub PDF for personal records, contractors, or small teams. Enter gross pay and deductions, and export a clear summary.

For official or regulated payroll, always confirm what your jurisdiction requires.

Paystub preview

The generated PDF can summarise gross pay, listed deductions, and the net amount owed for the selected period.

Word → PDF

Convert a Word document to PDF without needing a full office suite.

Conversion info

After the backend conversion runs, you’ll receive a direct download of the PDF. The layout depends on the original Word formatting.

PDF → Word

Turn a PDF back into an editable Word document so you can make changes or reuse the content elsewhere.

Conversion info

Depending on how the original PDF was created, text and formatting may convert more or less cleanly into Word.

Merge PDFs

Combine multiple PDFs into one file in the order you choose.

Merge overview

Your final PDF will contain all selected documents in sequence. In the future, this panel can show a page-order preview before you export.
