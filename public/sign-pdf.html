Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my signature?

You can draw your signature with a mouse or touchscreen, type your name and choose a signature font, or upload an image of your signature.

Are e-signatures legally binding?

Electronic signatures are legally recognized in most countries under laws like ESIGN (US) and eIDAS (EU). For specific legal questions, consult a legal professional.

Is my file safe?

Your PDF is processed on secure servers and deleted after your session. Your signature is never stored without your permission.
